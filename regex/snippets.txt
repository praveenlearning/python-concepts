
.       - Any character except new line
\d      - Digit (0-9)
\D      - Not a digit !(0-9)
\w      - Word character (a-z, A-Z, 0-9, _)
\W      - Not a word character
\s      - Whitespace (space, tab, new line)
\S      - Not whitespace (space, tab, new line)

\b      - Word boundary
\B      - Not a word boundary
^       - Beginning of line
$       - End of line

[]      - Characters in bracket
[^]     - Characters not in bracket
|       - Either or
()      - Group

Quantifiers

*       - 0 or more
+       - 1 or more
?       - 0 or 1
{n}     - Exact number
{m, n}  - Range of numbers (min, max)
